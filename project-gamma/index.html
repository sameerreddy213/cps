About CourseMap

CourseMap was born from the frustration of navigating complex academic requirements. We understand that planning your academic journey shouldn't feel like solving a puzzle blindfolded.

Our team of educators and developers created this tool to empower students with clear, visual representations of their academic pathways. Whether you're a freshman planning your first semester or a senior mapping out your final requirements, CourseMap makes academic planning intuitive and stress-free.

Join thousands of students who have already simplified their academic planning with CourseMap. Your future self will thank you.
